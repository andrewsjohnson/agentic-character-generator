# Hit Points

## HP at Level 1

```
Hit Point Maximum = Hit Die maximum value + Constitution modifier
```

| Class                                         | Hit Die | HP at Level 1          |
|-----------------------------------------------|---------|------------------------|
| Barbarian                                     | d12     | 12 + Con modifier      |
| Fighter, Paladin, Ranger                      | d10     | 10 + Con modifier      |
| Bard, Cleric, Druid, Monk, Rogue, Warlock     | d8      | 8 + Con modifier       |
| Sorcerer, Wizard                              | d6      | 6 + Con modifier       |

## HP on Level Up

Each level gained adds **1 Hit Die** to your pool. Increase HP maximum by one of:

- **Roll:** Roll the Hit Die + Constitution modifier (minimum of 1)
- **Fixed value:** Use the fixed amount below + Constitution modifier

| Class                                         | Fixed HP per Level |
|-----------------------------------------------|--------------------|
| Barbarian                                     | 7 + Con modifier   |
| Fighter, Paladin, Ranger                      | 6 + Con modifier   |
| Bard, Cleric, Druid, Monk, Rogue, Warlock     | 5 + Con modifier   |
| Sorcerer, Wizard                              | 4 + Con modifier   |

## Constitution Modifier Changes

If your Constitution modifier increases (e.g., from a feat), your HP maximum increases **retroactively** by 1 for each level you have attained.

**Example:** A level 8 character increases Constitution from 17 to 18 (modifier goes from +3 to +4). HP maximum increases by 8 in addition to the normal HP gained for the new level.

## Hit Dice Usage

- Hit Dice can be spent during a **Short Rest** to recover Hit Points.
- Roll spent Hit Dice + Constitution modifier to determine HP recovered.
- Track spent vs. available Hit Dice on your character sheet.

## Multiclass Hit Dice

Pool all Hit Dice from all classes. If the die types are the same, pool them together. If different, track them separately.

**Example:** Level 5 Fighter / Level 5 Paladin = 10d10 Hit Dice.
**Example:** Level 5 Cleric / Level 5 Paladin = 5d8 + 5d10 Hit Dice.
