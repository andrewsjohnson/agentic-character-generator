# Ability Scores

## The Six Abilities

Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma.

## Generation Methods

### Standard Array

Use these six scores: **15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8**

### Random Generation

Roll 4d6, drop the lowest die. Repeat six times.

### Point Buy

**Budget:** 27 points

| Score | Cost | Score | Cost |
|-------|------|-------|------|
| 8     | 0    | 12    | 4    |
| 9     | 1    | 13    | 5    |
| 10    | 2    | 14    | 7    |
| 11    | 3    | 15    | 9    |

Valid score range: **8–15** (before background adjustments).

## Background Adjustments

Your background lists three abilities. Choose one of:

- Increase one by **+2** and a different one by **+1**
- Increase all three by **+1**

No score can exceed **20** after adjustments.

## Ability Modifiers

| Score | Modifier | Score | Modifier |
|-------|----------|-------|----------|
| 3     | −4       | 12–13 | +1       |
| 4–5   | −3       | 14–15 | +2       |
| 6–7   | −2       | 16–17 | +3       |
| 8–9   | −1       | 18–19 | +4       |
| 10–11 | +0       | 20    | +5       |

**Formula:** `modifier = floor((score - 10) / 2)`
